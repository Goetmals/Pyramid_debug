DACs (greater than or equal to 10MSPS) Family Overview

The Digital-to-Analog converter (DAC) solutions from TI are primarily targeted towards Communications systems and Video / Imaging applications. The range of products include converter with 8, 10, 12 or 14 bits of resolution. Very low distortion performance and update rates of up to 200 MSPS make them ideal for the use in the transmit path of wireless communications systems. These high speed DACs can also be used in other applications such as cable modems, medical and test instrumentation. For Video/Imaging applications, integrated triple DACs are available to support component video processing.
