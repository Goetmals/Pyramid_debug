Video DACs

With unsurpassed performance and quality from the market leader, Analog Devices offers a wide range of Video DACs for all video applications.

Available in speed grades from 50 MHz, right up to 240 MHz, these products offer precision video in both 8-bit and 10-bit versions.

Single and triple DAC products are available to suit a variety of applications, including:

  • PC Graphics
  • Set-Top Boxes
  • Medical Imaging
  • Video Signal Generators
